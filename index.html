You are most welcome

Dear brothers and sisters in Christ, it is with great joy and pleasure that I extend a heartfelt welcome to all who have come to visit our parish website.

We believe it is no accident that you have found us; we believe that you are sent to us by God. Our parish strives to be a welcoming community that lives the Good News of the Gospel in its actions and programs. The parish is where we first encounter the Risen Lord in the Sacraments and receive the love and care of other people of faith. Whether you are a long-time member, new to the area, or simply exploring your faith, please know that our doors are always open, and you are a valued part of our extended family. We hope you will feel a sense of belonging and community here with us.

I invite you to explore this website to learn more about our:
  • Mass and Service Times: Find our current Mass Schedule and other worship opportunities.
  • Ministries and Programs: Discover ways to share your time and talent with us by exploring our various Parish Ministries and activities.
  • Sacramental Information: Learn about preparation for Baptism, First Communion, and other Sacraments.
  • Latest News & Events: Stay updated on upcoming events and community life through our Parish Calendar and news section.

Please feel at home and explore the website.

Fr. Kimbowa Charles

Fr. Kimbowa Charles has been serving at St. Charles Lwanga Jjinja Karoli Parish for 5 years. He specializes in youth ministry and community outreach programs.

He holds a degree in Theology from Makerere University and has a passion for social justice initiatives.

Fr. Raymond Ssegawa

Fr. Raymond Ssegawa joined our parish community 3 years ago. He focuses on adult faith formation and liturgical celebrations.

With a background in Canon Law, he also assists with parish administrative matters and sacramental preparation.
