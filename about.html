Our Story

St. Charles Lwanga Jjinja Karoli Parish was established in 1895 by a small group of immigrant families seeking to preserve their faith and cultural heritage. Over the decades, our parish has grown into a vibrant community serving over 2,000 families.

Our mission is to foster spiritual growth, serve our community, and share the love of Christ with all people. We strive to be a welcoming place where everyone can encounter God's grace and grow in their faith journey.

Throughout our history, we have remained committed to our founding principles of faith, service, and community. We continue to adapt to the changing needs of our members while staying true to our Catholic traditions.

Our Mission & Values

Faith

We nurture spiritual growth through prayer, sacraments, and religious education for all ages.

Service

We serve our community through outreach programs, charity work, and supporting those in need.

Community

We build relationships and foster a welcoming environment where everyone belongs.
